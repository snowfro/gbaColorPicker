Self Portrait

Create a pixel art self-portrait by selecting colors from the palettes and placing them on the canvas.

  1. Draw your full-body portrait
  2. Leave the background transparent (don't fill around your figure)
  3. Fill all pixels within your drawing (unless transparency is intentional)
  4. Grayscale colors are available at the end of each palette
  5. When finished, name your creation (pencil icon) and click "Download Bitmap String"
  6. Share via email or DM
